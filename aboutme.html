Hi, I'm Sabrina. My love for design started way back in the days of flogs and blogs around 2005, when I spent hours customizing layouts just for fun. It was my creative escape, and I never imagined it would turn into a career. Fast forward to 2017, I finally decided to take the leap and started working professionally as a UI Designer.

Since then, I’ve been all about creating designs that don’t just look good but also work great. A few years in, I got curious about how those designs were actually built, and that’s how I got into web development. Now, I’m diving deep into JavaScript and React, combining my design background with coding to bring ideas to life on the web.

Let's work together!
